Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how Seasonbase processes personal data in connection with our waitlist and future launch.

1. Data Controller

Seasonbase is the data controller for the processing of your personal data.

If you have any questions about how we process your personal data, you can contact us at: hello@seasonbase.net

2. What Data We Collect

When you sign up for our waitlist, we may collect the following information:

name, email address, selected destinations, what you are looking for — job, housing or both — and any other information you voluntarily provide to us.

3. Why We Collect the Data

We process your personal data in order to:

manage our waitlist, contact you about Seasonbase, provide information about the launch, early access and relevant updates, and understand which destinations and needs are most in demand.

4. Legal Basis

We process your personal data based on your consent when you actively agree to this in the form.

5. How Long We Keep the Data

We keep your personal data for as long as it is needed to manage the waitlist and our planned launch, or until you withdraw your consent and ask us to delete your data.

If the data is no longer relevant for this purpose, we will delete it.

6. Sharing of Personal Data

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

We may share data with service providers that help us operate the service, such as hosting providers, email tools or other technical service providers, to the extent necessary to provide Seasonbase.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request information about what personal data we hold about you, request correction of inaccurate data, request deletion of your data, and withdraw your consent.

To exercise your rights, contact us at: hello@seasonbase.net

8. Security

We work to protect your personal data through reasonable technical and organisational security measures.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy when needed. The latest version will always be available on this page.
